ABOUT

WALANG TAKOT, KUNDI PASASALAMAT

Even if I go through the deepest darkness, I will not be afraid, Lord, for you are with me. Your shepherd’s rod and staff protect me. – Psalm 23:4, Good News Bible

Lumakad man ako sa lambak ng karimlan, wala akong katatakutang masama pagkat kasama kita. Ang pamalo mo’t tungkod ang magpapasigla sa akin. – Awit 23:4, Biblia ng Sambayanang Pilipino

This book is a compilation of the person stories of Beth Dela Cruz -- a Catholic community servant, businesswoman, former OFW, and cancer survivor - recalling how wonderful God's grace and provisions are to her life.

Sa lahat ng pagkakataon sa kanyang buhay-paghihirap, kasaganaan, pagsubok, sakit, takot, at pangamba- ang biyaya ng Diyos ay lubos at walang katapusan. Sa librong ito, papuri at pasasalamat ang alay ni Beth sa Panginoon.

Thank You, Thank You, Lord! invites every reader to look at their own life and be grateful for God's blessings in every situation.

AUTHOR

Lilibeth Mejia Dela Cruz

Lilibeth Dela Cruz, or Beth for short, is an accountancy graduate at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. She is an active intercessory ministry servant of the Light of Jesus Family. She also serves as lector and commentator at Our Lady of Guadalupe Parish in Bulelak, Marikina City.

After having past careers in information technology, she now has turned to mixing inspired perfume as her business

He journey against breast cancer is her greatest inspiration to write this book on gratitude and thanksgiving.
